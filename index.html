Meet TESSA

Your AI-powered Technical Equipment Support & Service Assistant for biomedical technology

What is TESSA?

TESSA is an advanced AI assistant designed for biomedical technicians and healthcare professionals who work with medical equipment. Using natural language processing and machine learning, TESSA helps diagnose technical issues, recommend service options, and provide guidance for maintaining critical devices.

Key Features

Rapid Diagnostics

Quickly identify issues based on symptoms and error codes.

Procedural Guidance

Step-by-step instructions for calibration and repairs.

Equipment Database

Access to specs, common issues, and maintenance schedules.

Smart Recommendations

Service suggestions based on symptoms and device history.

How TESSA Works

1. Describe Your Equipment Issue

Include any error codes, symptoms, or behavior in plain English.

2. TESSA Analyzes the Information

Uses its database to understand and match your problem.

3. Receive Expert Guidance

Get step-by-step repair instructions or service advice.

4. Implement the Solution

Resolve the issue or get referred to a trusted technician.
